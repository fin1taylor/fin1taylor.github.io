Daily Design Challenges
Project Overview
Daily UI is an email subscription service that sends you a UI design prompt for 100 weekdays straight. Daily UI helped me improve my design skills because it was constantly inspiring me to overcome new design challenges. Here are just a few of my designs but you can see the rest of them on Dribbble.
Day 2 - Checkout: The prompt was to design a credit card checkout page and was one of the first designs I made for Daily UI.
Day 18 - Steps: The prompt was to design an analytics chart so I decided to create a health app that displayed the user's number of steps in the form of a graph throughout the day.
Day 30 - Wallet: The prompt was to design something related to pricing so I decided to make a wallet app interface.
For some of the designs, I tried to design for different platforms. These three were made for the apple watch.
Day 14 - Countdown Timer: I wanted the timer to still have the classic round watch feel so the "time left" is surrounded by a circular line that decreases as the timer goes down.
Day 21 - Home Monitoring Dashboard: I designed a simple interface for scrolling through and viewing your home's basic information such as which lights are on and what the ambient temperature is.
Day 29 - Map Navigation Interface: I figured that trying to view a full map on the apple watch wouldn't make for an enjoyable user experience. Instead, I decided to simply display the navigation information such as "In 100 ft, Take a Left".
